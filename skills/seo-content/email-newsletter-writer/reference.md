## Email Copywriting

### The Perfect Email Structure

```
[SUBJECT LINE: 40-60 chars]

[PREHEADER: 50-100 chars]

[HOOK: Opening 2-3 lines]

[BODY: Value delivery]
  - Subheading 1
  - Content
  - Subheading 2
  - Content

[CTA: Clear action]

[CLOSING: Brief sign-off]

[P.S.: Second hook/CTA]
```

### Subject Line Psychology

**What makes people open:**

1. **Curiosity** - Gap between what they know and want to know
2. **Urgency** - Time-sensitive information
3. **Value** - Clear benefit stated
4. **Personal** - Uses their name or relevant info
5. **Social** - What others are doing

### Subject Line Formulas

**Curiosity:**
```
The surprising truth about [topic]
What [successful people] know about [topic]
I was wrong about [common belief]
```

**Urgency:**
```
[X hours] left to [benefit]
Don't miss this (expires tonight)
Final reminder: [offer]
```

**Value:**
```
How to [achieve goal] in [timeframe]
[Number] ways to [benefit]
Your guide to [topic]
```

**Personal:**
```
[Name], your weekly [topic] update
A personal note about [topic]
Quick question, [Name]
```

### Body Copy Best Practices

**Opening hooks:**
- Personal story
- Surprising statistic
- Provocative question
- Bold statement
- Relatable struggle

**Transitions:**
- "Here's the thing..."
- "But wait, there's more..."
- "Now, I know what you're thinking..."
- "Let me explain..."
- "Here's why this matters..."

**Closing patterns:**
- Summary of key points
- Single clear CTA
- Future teaser
- Personal sign-off
- P.S. with value

## Deliverability

### Sender Reputation Factors

| Factor | Impact | How to Maintain |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Bounce rate | High | Clean list regularly |
| Spam complaints | High | Easy unsubscribe |
| Open rate | Medium | Quality content |
| Click rate | Medium | Relevant offers |
| List growth | Low | Organic signups |

### Spam Trigger Words

**Avoid or use sparingly:**

Money/Finance:
- Free, $$, Cash, Discount, Save
- No cost, No obligation, No credit

Urgency/Hype:
- Act now, Limited time, Urgent
- Call now, Don't hesitate, Offer expires

Claims:
- Guaranteed, 100%, Incredible, Amazing
- Once in a lifetime, You won't believe

### Technical Setup

**Required:**
- SPF record configured
- DKIM signing enabled
- DMARC policy set
- Valid reply-to address
- Physical address in footer

**Best Practices:**
- Warm up new domains
- Consistent sender name
- Consistent from address
- Monitor blacklists
- Regular list cleaning

## Segmentation

### Segmentation Criteria

| Type | Examples | Use Case |
|------|----------|----------|
| Demographic | Age, location, job | Personalization |
| Behavioral | Opens, clicks, purchases | Engagement |
| Lifecycle | New, active, lapsed | Re-engagement |
| Preference | Topics, frequency | Content matching |

### Common Segments

**Engagement-based:**
```
Super engaged: Opened 5+ of last 5
Engaged: Opened 2-4 of last 5
At risk: Opened 1 of last 5
Inactive: Opened 0 of last 5
```

**Purchase-based:**
```
Buyers: Made purchase
High-value: Multiple purchases
Recent: Purchased last 30 days
Lapsed: No purchase in 90+ days
```

### Segmentation Strategies

**New subscribers:**
- Welcome sequence
- Onboarding content
- Early engagement

**Power users:**
- Exclusive content
- Early access
- Community building

**At-risk:**
- Re-engagement campaigns
- Win-back offers
- Feedback requests

## Automation

### Essential Email Sequences

**Welcome Series (5 emails):**
```
Day 0: Welcome + quick win
Day 2: Your story/mission
Day 4: Best content piece
Day 7: Product introduction
Day 10: Social proof + offer
```

**Abandoned Cart (3 emails):**
```
Hour 1: Reminder (no discount)
Hour 24: Value reinforcement
Hour 48: Final reminder + incentive
```

**Re-engagement (3 emails):**
```
Email 1: "We miss you"
Email 2: "Here's what you missed"
Email 3: "Last chance" + offer
```

### Automation Best Practices

**Triggers:**
- Signup → Welcome series
- No open 30 days → Re-engagement
- Click on X → Related content
- Purchase → Thank you + upsell
- Birthday → Special offer

**Timing:**
- Respect time zones
- Avoid sending at night
- Space emails appropriately
- Test different times

## Analytics

### Key Metrics to Track

| Metric | Formula | Benchmark |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| Open Rate | Opens / Delivered | 20-25% |
| Click Rate | Clicks / Delivered | 2-4% |
| CTOR | Clicks / Opens | 10-15% |
| Unsubscribe | Unsubs / Delivered | <0.5% |
| Bounce | Bounces / Sent | <2% |

### What Metrics Mean

**High opens, low clicks:**
- Subject line working
- Content not compelling
- CTA not clear
- Links not visible

**Low opens, high clicks:**
- Subject line weak
- Engaged audience
- Right content for wrong people
- Consider re-send to non-openers

**High unsubscribes:**
- Wrong frequency
- Wrong content
- Poor expectation setting
- List quality issues

### Optimization Framework

1. **Subject lines** - Test variations
2. **Send time** - Test different times
3. **Content length** - Test short vs long
4. **CTA placement** - Test above/below fold
5. **Personalization** - Test level of customization

## Mobile Optimization

### Mobile Email Stats

- 60%+ emails opened on mobile
- 3 seconds to decide to read
- Thumb-zone for CTAs

### Mobile Best Practices

**Subject lines:**
- Under 30 chars (visible on mobile)
- Front-load key words

**Design:**
- Single column layout
- Large fonts (14px+ body)
- Tap-friendly buttons (44px+)
- Adequate white space

**Content:**
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Clear hierarchy
- Important content first
- Limited images

## Legal Compliance

### CAN-SPAM Requirements

- Clear identification of sender
- Accurate subject lines
- Physical address included
- Easy unsubscribe option
- Honor opt-outs promptly

### GDPR Requirements

- Explicit consent
- Right to be forgotten
- Data portability
- Record of consent
- Privacy policy link

### Best Practices

- Double opt-in
- Clear expectations at signup
- Preference center
- Regular re-confirmation
- Easy unsubscribe

## Email Types Reference

### Weekly Newsletter

**Structure:**
```
Subject: Your weekly [topic] roundup

Hey [Name],

[Opening hook - what's new/important]

This week:
• [Item 1 with brief description]
• [Item 2 with brief description]
• [Item 3 with brief description]

[Primary CTA]

[Optional: Quick tip or insight]

Until next week,
[Signature]

P.S. [Bonus content or reminder]
```

### Product Update

**Structure:**
```
Subject: New: [Feature name] is here

[Name],

We've got something new for you.

[Feature Name]
[Brief description of what it does]

Why you'll love it:
✓ [Benefit 1]
✓ [Benefit 2]
✓ [Benefit 3]

[Screenshot or GIF]

[CTA: Try it now]

Questions? Just reply to this email.

[Signature]
```

**Structure:**
```
Subject: How to [achieve specific outcome]

[Name],

[Hook: Problem or desire statement]

[Main content - educational value]

Step 1: [Action]
[Explanation]

Step 2: [Action]
[Explanation]

Step 3: [Action]
[Explanation]

[Key takeaway]

[CTA related to learning]

[Signature]

P.S. [Related resource]
```
