```
- What I learned after [X years/experience]
- This changed everything for me
- I was wrong about [common belief]
- [X] things nobody tells you about [topic]
```

### Value
```
- How to [achieve goal] in [timeframe]
- [X] [tips/strategies/tools] for [outcome]
- Your guide to [topic]
- The complete [topic] checklist
- [X] lessons from [experience/person]
```

### Personal
```
- Can I be honest with you?
- A confession...
- I made a mistake
- Quick question for you
- [Name], I noticed something
```

```
- [X hours] left
- Don't miss this
- Closing soon: [offer]
- Last chance for [benefit]
- Reminder: [deadline]
```

```
- Everyone's talking about this
- What [X] people know about [topic]
- Join [X]+ [people type]
- The #1 [thing] in [category]
```
