If you've ever [common frustration]...

Or wondered why [problem persists]...

Then this might be the most important letter you read all year.

Here's why:
```

```

Let me guess...

You've tried [solution 1]. It didn't work because [reason].

You've tried [solution 2]. That failed because [reason].

You've even tried [solution 3]. And while it helped a little, [limitation].

And until you address [root cause], nothing will change.

[Pause to let that sink in]

```

```

```
Tired of [Problem]? Here's the Fix.
Stop [Pain Point]. Start [Benefit].
[Problem] Is Costing You [Consequence]. Here's the Solution.
```
