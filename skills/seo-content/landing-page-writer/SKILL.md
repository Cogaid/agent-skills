## Purpose

Write compelling landing page copy that captures attention, communicates value, and drives visitors to take action.

## Quick Reference

### Landing Page Types

| Type | Goal | Length | Key Elements |
|------|------|--------|--------------|
| Lead Gen | Email capture | Short | Hook, value, form |
| Sales | Purchase | Long | Story, proof, urgency |
| Product | Signup/trial | Medium | Features, benefits, CTA |
| Event | Registration | Medium | Details, speakers, FOMO |
| App | Download | Short | Value prop, screenshots |

### Conversion Benchmarks

| Page Type | Good | Great |
|-----------|------|-------|
| Lead Gen | 20%+ | 40%+ |
| Free Trial | 10%+ | 25%+ |

| Event | 15%+ | 30%+ |

## Page Structure

### The AIDA Framework

| Section | Purpose | Word Count |
|---------|---------|------------|
| **A**ttention | Hook headline | 5-10 words |
| **I**nterest | Problem/desire | 50-100 words |
| **D**esire | Solution/benefits | 150-300 words |
| **A**ction | CTA | 3-7 words |

## Copy Elements

### Headlines That Convert

**Formulas:**
- How to [Get Desired Result] without [Pain Point]
- The [Adjective] Way to [Achieve Goal]
- [Number] [Things] That [Outcome]
- Stop [Problem]. Start [Solution].
- [Result] in [Timeframe] Guaranteed

### Benefit Bullets

**Format:**
- [Feature] → [Benefit] → [Outcome]

**Example:**
- One-click export → Save hours on reports → Spend time on strategy

### Social Proof Types

- Customer testimonials
- Company logos
- Statistics/metrics
- Media mentions
- Certifications

## Conversion Killers

Avoid these mistakes:
- Multiple competing CTAs
- Unclear value proposition
- Wall of text
- No social proof
- Slow load time
- Friction in forms
- Generic stock images
- No urgency/scarcity

## Testing Guidelines

### A/B Test Priority

1. **Headlines** - Biggest impact
2. **CTA button** - Text and color
3. **Hero image** - Visual appeal
4. **Social proof** - Placement
5. **Form fields** - Less is more
