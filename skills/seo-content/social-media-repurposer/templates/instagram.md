
## Reels Script Templates

### Quick Tips Reel (15-30 sec)
```
HOOK (0-3 sec):
[On screen]: "[X] [topic] tips you need"
[Voice]: "Here are [X] [topic] tips that actually work"

CONTENT (3-25 sec):
Tip 1: [Text on screen + quick voiceover]
Tip 2: [Text on screen + quick voiceover]
Tip 3: [Text on screen + quick voiceover]

CTA (25-30 sec):
[On screen]: "Follow for more"
[Voice]: "Follow for more tips like this"
```

### Before/After Reel
```
HOOK (0-3 sec):
[On screen]: "Before vs After [doing thing]"

BEFORE (3-10 sec):
[Show the "before" state]
[Text]: "Before: [description]"

AFTER (10-20 sec):
[Show the "after" state]
[Text]: "After: [description]"

REVEAL (20-30 sec):
[Show what changed]
"Here's what I did..."

CTA:
"Save this for later"
```

### Day in the Life
```
HOOK (0-3 sec):
"A day in my life as a [role]"

CONTENT (3-50 sec):
[Morning routine]
[Key work activities]
[Challenges faced]
[Wins/accomplishments]
[End of day]

CTA (50-60 sec):
"Follow for more behind the scenes"
```

## Best Practices

### Carousel Tips
- Cover slide must grab attention
- One point per slide
- Consistent design/branding
- End with clear CTA

### Caption Tips
- Hook in first line
- Use line breaks
- Emojis as visual breaks
- 20-30 hashtags in comments (or 3-5 in caption)

### Reels Tips
- Hook in first 1-2 seconds
- Add captions/text
- Use trending audio
- 15-30 seconds performs best
