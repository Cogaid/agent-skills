## Best Practices

### Formatting
- First line is your hook (visible before "see more")
- Use line breaks liberally
- Symbols for bullet points: → ↳ • ✓ ✗
- 3-5 hashtags at the end
- End with a question or CTA

### Content Tips
- Personal stories outperform generic advice
- First-person perspective builds connection
- Specific numbers beat vague claims
- Vulnerability builds trust

### Engagement Tips
- Respond to every comment in first hour
- Ask questions in comments to continue discussion
- Tag relevant people when appropriate
- Repost your best content after 3+ months
