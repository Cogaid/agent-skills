## Writing Tips for Listicles

### Number Selection
- Odd numbers (7, 9, 11) can perform well
- "10" and "5" are classics
- Large numbers (50+) suggest comprehensiveness
- Small numbers (3-5) suggest focus

### Formatting Tips
- Consistent structure for each item
- Use subheadings within items
- Add comparison tables
- Include visuals per item
- Keep individual items scannable

### SEO Tips
- Include number in title tag
- Year in title if time-sensitive
- Use list schema markup
- Each H2 can target a secondary keyword
