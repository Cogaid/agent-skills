## Tips for All Post Types

1. **Hook in first paragraph** - Don't bury the value
2. **Subheadings for scannability** - Most readers skim
3. **One idea per section** - Don't overload
4. **Examples everywhere** - Abstract → Concrete
5. **End with action** - What should they do next?
