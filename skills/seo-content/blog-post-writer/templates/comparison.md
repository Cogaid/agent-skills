## Writing Tips for Comparison Posts

### Objectivity
- Present balanced information
- Back claims with evidence
- Acknowledge trade-offs
- Let readers draw conclusions

### Structure
- Start with quick verdict
- Use comparison tables liberally
- Include clear recommendations
- Add alternatives section

### SEO
- "[A] vs [B]" in title
- Include both product names
- Add comparison schema
- Target commercial intent keywords
