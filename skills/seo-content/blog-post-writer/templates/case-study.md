## Writing Tips for Case Studies

### Storytelling
- Follow narrative arc (challenge → solution → results)
- Include specific details and numbers
- Use client quotes throughout
- Make it relatable to target audience

### Credibility
- Use real names and companies (with permission)
- Include specific, verifiable metrics
- Add visuals (photos, charts, screenshots)
- Include timeline details

### SEO & Conversion
- Target "[industry/solution] case study" keywords
- Include company/industry in title
- Add clear CTAs throughout
- Link to related services/solutions
