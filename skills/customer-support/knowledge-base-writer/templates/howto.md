## Example: Password Reset

```markdown
# How to reset your password

Forgot your password? Reset it in under a minute using your email.

## Before you start

Make sure you have:
- Access to the email address on your account
- A few minutes to complete the process

## Steps

### 1. Go to the login page

Navigate to [yourapp.com/login](https://yourapp.com/login).

### 2. Click "Forgot password?"

Find the link below the password field and click it.

### 3. Enter your email address

Type the email address associated with your account and click **Send Reset Link**.

**Tip**: Check your spam folder if you don't see the email within 2 minutes.

### 4. Check your email

Open the password reset email and click **Reset Password**.

### 5. Create a new password

Enter your new password twice and click **Save**.

**Requirements**: At least 8 characters with one number and one special character.

## Done!

You can now log in with your new password.

## Troubleshooting

**I didn't get the reset email**
Check your spam folder. If still not there, try again or contact support.

**The reset link says "expired"**
Links expire after 24 hours. Request a new one from the login page.

## Related articles

- [How to enable two-factor authentication]
- [How to update your email address]
```

## Writing Tips

1. **Title**: Start with "How to" + action verb
2. **First line**: Single sentence, states the outcome
3. **Prerequisites**: Only include what's truly needed
4. **Steps**: One action per step, number them
5. **Verbs**: Start each step with an action verb
6. **Screenshots**: Use sparingly, only when UI isn't obvious
7. **Troubleshooting**: Add 2-3 common issues
