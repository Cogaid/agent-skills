## Escalation Acknowledgment

```
Subject: Personal attention to your case [Ticket #]

Hi [Name],

Thank you for bringing this to my attention. I can see this has been
frustrating, and I want you to know I'm taking personal ownership of
resolving this for you.

I've reviewed your case history, and I understand:
- [Summary point 1]
- [Summary point 2]
- [Summary point 3]

That's not the experience you should have had with us, and I apologize.

Here's what I'm doing right now:
1. [Immediate action]
2. [Follow-up action with timeline]

I'll personally follow up with you by [specific time/date] with an update.
If you need to reach me before then, reply directly to this email.

Best,
[Agent Name]
[Title/Team]
```

## Resolution With Apology

```
Subject: Re: [Original Subject] - Issue Resolved [Ticket #]

Hi [Name],

I want to start by apologizing for [specific issue]. You trusted us
with [your business/workflow/data], and we let you down.

Here's what happened:
[Brief, honest explanation - not an excuse]

Here's what we've done to fix it:
1. [Resolution action 1]
2. [Resolution action 2]
3. [Prevention measure for future]

[If compensation applicable]:
To make things right, I've also [compensation action].

Your feedback on this issue is valuable, and we're using it to
[improvement we're making]. Thank you for giving us the chance to
make this right.

Is there anything else I can help you with?

Best,
[Agent Name]
```

## Compensation Offer

```
Subject: Making things right [Ticket #]

Hi [Name],

I've been reviewing your recent experience with us, and I want to
make things right.

What happened:
[Brief summary of issue]

I know this impacted [specific impact on them], and for that I'm sorry.

To compensate for the inconvenience, I'd like to offer:
[Compensation option 1]

[If multiple options]:
Alternatively, I can [option 2] if that would be more valuable to you.

Just reply to let me know which you'd prefer, or if you have
questions about either option.

We value your business and appreciate your patience as we resolved this.

Best,
[Agent Name]
```

## Manager Follow-Up

```
Subject: Follow-up from [Company] management [Ticket #]

Hi [Name],

My name is [Manager Name], and I'm [Title] at [Company]. [Agent Name]
shared your recent experience with me, and I wanted to reach out personally.

First, I want to apologize. [Specific issue] is not the standard we
hold ourselves to, and I understand how this affected your [workflow/
business/team].

I've looked into what happened, and here's what we're doing:

1. Immediate: [What's been done]
2. Prevention: [How we're preventing recurrence]
3. For you: [Any additional compensation/gesture]

Your feedback is helping us improve, and I want to thank you for
bringing this to our attention.

If you have any concerns or would like to discuss further, please
reply directly to this email or schedule time with me: [Calendar link]

Best regards,
[Manager Name]
[Title]
[Direct phone number]
```

## Service Recovery

```
Subject: We want to make this right [Ticket #]

Hi [Name],

I know your recent experience with [Company] hasn't been what you expected,
and I want to personally ensure we make this right.

I understand you've experienced:
- [Issue 1]
- [Issue 2]
- [Issue 3]

You shouldn't have had to deal with any of this, and I'm sorry.

Here's my commitment to you:

1. **[Immediate resolution]**: This has been completed as of [date/time]
2. **[Compensation]**: I've applied [specific compensation] to your account
3. **[Follow-up]**: I will personally check in with you on [date] to ensure
   everything is working smoothly
4. **[Single point of contact]**: Moving forward, you can reach me directly at
   [email] for any issues

Is there anything else that would help restore your confidence in us?

Best,
[Agent Name]
[Direct contact info]
```

## Cannot Accommodate

```
Subject: Re: [Original Subject] [Ticket #]

Hi [Name],

Thank you for your patience as I looked into this.

I understand you'd like [their specific request], and I wish I could
make that happen. Unfortunately, [honest explanation without blame or
excessive detail].

Here's what I can do instead:
- [Alternative 1]
- [Alternative 2]

Would either of these work for your situation?

I know this isn't the answer you were hoping for, and I appreciate
your understanding. If you'd like to discuss other options, I'm
happy to continue the conversation.

Best,
[Agent Name]
```

## Churn Prevention

```
Subject: We'd hate to see you go [Ticket #]

Hi [Name],

I understand you're considering canceling your account, and I want to
make sure we've done everything we can to address your concerns first.

From what I understand, the main issues are:
- [Issue 1]
- [Issue 2]

I've looked into what we can do, and I'd like to offer:
[Specific resolution or offer]

Additionally, I want to share some updates you might have missed:
- [Recent improvement relevant to their pain point]
- [Upcoming feature addressing their concern]

Would you be open to a quick call to discuss? I'm confident we can
find a solution that works for you.

If you've already made your decision, I understand. I'd just appreciate
the chance to hear your feedback so we can improve for others.

Best,
[Agent Name]
[Calendar link for call]
```

## Post-Resolution Check-In

```
Subject: Checking in [Ticket #]

Hi [Name],

I wanted to follow up on the issue we resolved last week regarding
[brief summary].

How's everything working now? I want to make sure the solution is
holding up and you're not experiencing any other issues.

If anything needs adjustment or you have other questions, just reply
to this email and I'll take care of it.

Thanks again for your patience while we resolved this.

Best,
[Agent Name]
```

## Variables Reference

| Variable | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| `[Name]` | Customer's first name |
| `[Ticket #]` | Support ticket ID |
| `[Agent Name]` | Your name |
| `[Title]` | Your role/title |
| `[Company]` | Your company name |
| `[Calendar link]` | Scheduling link |
