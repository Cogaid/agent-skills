# Refund Communication Templates

## Approval Templates

### Full Refund Approved

**Email:**
```
Subject: Your Refund Has Been Processed - Order #[ORDER_NUMBER]

Hi [CUSTOMER_NAME],

Great news! We've processed your refund for Order #[ORDER_NUMBER].

Refund Details:
• Amount: $[AMOUNT]
• Method: [ORIGINAL_PAYMENT_METHOD]
• Expected Timeline: [3-5 business days]

Reference Number: [REFUND_ID]

The refund will appear on your [payment method] statement within [timeframe]. Please note that your bank may take additional time to post the credit.

If you have any questions, reply to this email or contact us at [SUPPORT_EMAIL].

Thank you for your patience,
[AGENT_NAME]
[COMPANY_NAME] Customer Support
```

**Chat/Phone:**
```
I've processed your full refund of $[AMOUNT] for Order #[ORDER_NUMBER].

You'll see this back on your [payment method] within [3-5] business days. Your refund reference number is [REFUND_ID].

Is there anything else I can help you with?
```

## Denial Templates

### Standard Denial

**Email:**
```
Subject: Regarding Your Refund Request - Order #[ORDER_NUMBER]

Hi [CUSTOMER_NAME],

Thank you for reaching out about Order #[ORDER_NUMBER].

After reviewing your request, we're unable to process a refund at this time because [REASON - e.g., "the item was purchased more than 30 days ago, which is outside our return window"].

What You Can Do:
• [ALTERNATIVE 1 - e.g., "Use your manufacturer warranty"]
• [ALTERNATIVE 2 - e.g., "Sell through our marketplace"]
• [ALTERNATIVE 3 - e.g., "Donate for tax credit"]

For reference, our return policy can be found at [POLICY_LINK].

If you believe this decision was made in error or have additional information to share, please reply to this email and we'll review your case again.

Sincerely,
[AGENT_NAME]
[COMPANY_NAME] Customer Support
```

**Chat/Phone:**
```
I've looked into this, and unfortunately, I'm not able to process a refund because [REASON].

However, here's what I can offer:
• [ALTERNATIVE 1]
• [ALTERNATIVE 2]

Would either of those help?

[If customer pushes back]: I understand this isn't the answer you were hoping for. If you'd like, I can have a supervisor review your case for any possible exceptions.
```

### Outside Return Window

**Email:**
```
Subject: Your Return Request - Order #[ORDER_NUMBER]

Hi [CUSTOMER_NAME],

Thank you for contacting us about Order #[ORDER_NUMBER] from [ORDER_DATE].

We understand you'd like to return this item, but unfortunately, it's outside our [30-day] return window. Our policy requires returns to be initiated within [30 days] of delivery.

Your order was delivered on: [DELIVERY_DATE]
Return window closed on: [WINDOW_END_DATE]

While we can't accept a return, here are some options:
• [If manufacturer warranty applies]: Contact [MANUFACTURER] at [CONTACT] for warranty support
• [If resale possible]: List on [MARKETPLACE]
• [If upgradeable]: Upgrade to our latest version at [DISCOUNT]%

We value your business and hope to serve you better next time.

Best regards,
[AGENT_NAME]
[COMPANY_NAME] Customer Support
```

### Final Sale Item

```
Subject: Your Inquiry About Order #[ORDER_NUMBER]

Hi [CUSTOMER_NAME],

Thank you for reaching out about your recent purchase.

The item(s) you ordered were marked as "Final Sale" at the time of purchase, which means they're not eligible for returns or refunds. This was indicated:
• On the product page
• In your cart
• On your order confirmation

We completely understand this can be frustrating. If there's a defect or quality issue with the item, please let us know - we may be able to help with that separately.

Thank you for your understanding,
[AGENT_NAME]
```
