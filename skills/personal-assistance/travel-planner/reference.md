## Time Zone Management

### Jet Lag Strategies

**Eastbound travel** (harder - losing hours):
- Shift sleep earlier 2-3 days before
- Seek morning light on arrival
- Avoid naps first day
- Light dinner, early bedtime
- Stay hydrated

**Westbound travel** (easier - gaining hours):
- Shift sleep later before trip
- Seek evening light on arrival
- Short nap okay (<20 min)
- Stay up until local bedtime
- Stay hydrated

### General Tips
- Adjust watch to destination time on departure
- Sleep on the plane if it's night at destination
- Avoid alcohol and caffeine on flight
- Move around during flight
- Stay hydrated (1 glass water per hour)

## Packing Guidelines

### Carry-On Only (1-4 days)

**Bag size**: Under 22" x 14" x 9"

**Clothing**:
- 1 outfit per day (mix & match)
- 1 backup top
- Wear bulkiest items
- Shoes: 2 pairs max

**System**:
- Packing cubes for organization
- Roll clothes to save space
- Toiletries in TSA-approved bag

### Checked Bag (5+ days)

**All carry-on items plus**:
- Additional outfits
- Full-size toiletries
- Backup charger/accessories
- Weather-specific items

### International Additions
- Passport (6+ months validity)
- Visa documentation
- Power adapters
- Local currency
- Travel insurance docs
- Copies of documents (separate)

## Emergency Procedures

### Common Issues

| Issue | Immediate Action |
|-------|-----------------|
| Flight cancelled | Go to desk + call airline simultaneously |
| Lost luggage | File claim before leaving airport |
| Lost passport | Contact embassy immediately |
| Medical emergency | Hotel can assist; have insurance ready |
| Missed connection | Airline rebooks; document everything |

### Emergency Contact Card

Keep on phone + printed copy:

```
EMERGENCY INFO
--------------
Name: [Your name]
Blood type: [If known]
Allergies: [List]
Medications: [List]
Emergency contact: [Name, Phone]
Travel insurance: [Policy #, Phone]
Embassy: [Phone, Address]
Hotel: [Name, Phone, Address]
```

## Pre-Travel Checklist

### 1 Week Before
- [ ] Confirm all reservations
- [ ] Check passport/visa validity
- [ ] Arrange pet/plant care
- [ ] Schedule mail hold
- [ ] Download offline maps
- [ ] Check weather forecast
- [ ] Notify bank of travel
- [ ] Check phone international plan

### Day Before
- [ ] Check in online for flight
- [ ] Download boarding pass
- [ ] Pack bag using checklist
- [ ] Charge all devices
- [ ] Confirm ground transportation
- [ ] Set alarm with buffer
- [ ] Share itinerary with emergency contact

### Travel Day
- [ ] Arrive airport 2 hours early (3 intl)
- [ ] Keep essentials in personal item
- [ ] Stay hydrated
- [ ] Confirm arrival transportation
