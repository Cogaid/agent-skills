## Tips for Weekly Planning

1. **Do it consistently** - Same time each week
2. **Start with Big 3** - Everything else flows from there
3. **Block the Big 3 first** - Before other meetings
4. **Leave buffer** - 40% of time for unexpected
5. **Review weekly** - Learn what works for you
