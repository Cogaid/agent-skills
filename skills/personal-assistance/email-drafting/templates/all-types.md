# Email Templates

## Request Email

```
Subject: Request: [Specific thing needed]

Hi [Name],

I need [specific request] for [reason/project].

Specifically:
- [Item 1]
- [Item 2]
- [Item 3]

Could you send this by [specific date]? If that doesn't work,
let me know what would.

Thanks,
[Name]
```

---

## Follow-Up Email

```
Subject: Following up: [Original topic]

Hi [Name],

Following up on [original topic/email sent on date].

[One-sentence context reminder]

Is there anything you need from me to move forward?

Thanks,
[Name]
```

---

## Introduction Email

```
Subject: Intro: [Name 1] <> [Name 2]

Hi [Name 1] and [Name 2],

I wanted to connect you two.

[Name 1], meet [Name 2]. [1-2 sentences about Name 2].

[Name 2], meet [Name 1]. [1-2 sentences about Name 1].

I'll leave you to connect. Moving to BCC.

Best,
[Name]
```

---

## Thank You Email

```
Subject: Thank you for [specific thing]

Hi [Name],

Thank you for [specific action they took].

[Brief note on impact/why it mattered]

[Next steps if applicable]

Thanks again,
[Name]
```

---

## Apology Email

```
Subject: [Direct acknowledgment of situation]

Hi [Name],

I owe you an apology. [Direct statement of what went wrong]

[Brief explanation - not an excuse]

Here's what I'm doing to fix it:
- [Action 1]
- [Action 2]

I'm sorry for [specific impact on them]. Let me know if you
have questions.

[Name]
```

---

## Decline Email

```
Subject: Re: [Their original subject]

Hi [Name],

Thank you for [their request/offer].

I'm not able to [what they asked] because [brief reason].

[Alternative suggestion if appropriate]

I appreciate you thinking of me.

Best,
[Name]
```

## Meeting Request

```
Subject: Meeting request: [Topic]

Hi [Name],

I'd like to schedule time to discuss [topic]. The goal is
[specific decision/outcome].

Expected duration: [length]

Times that work for me:
- [Option 1]
- [Option 2]
- [Option 3]

Let me know what works, or suggest alternatives.

Thanks,
[Name]
```

## Cold Outreach

```
Subject: [Personalized, intriguing - 3-5 words]

Hi [Name],

[Personalized opening about them/their company]

[Problem they likely face]

We've helped [similar company] achieve [result].

Would you be open to a 15-minute call to explore if we could
help [their company] [achieve outcome]?

[Name]
[Title]
```

[Name]

## Announcement

```

- [Key point 1]

- [Key point 3]

WHAT THIS MEANS FOR YOU:
[Brief explanation of impact]

[What they need to do, if anything]

[Name]
```

## Handoff Email

```
Subject: Handoff: [Project/Account] to [New Owner]

Hi [All parties],

I'm handing off [project/account] to [New Owner] effective [date].

BACKGROUND:
[Brief context on what this is]

CURRENT STATUS:
- [Status point 1]
- [Status point 2]

KEY CONTACTS:
- [Name]: [Role]
- [Name]: [Role]

OUTSTANDING ITEMS:
- [Item 1]
- [Item 2]

[New Owner] will be your point of contact going forward.

[Current Owner]
```

## Out of Office

```
Subject: Out of office: [Date] - [Date]

Hi,

Thanks for your email. I'm out of the office from [start date]
to [end date] with limited email access.

For urgent matters, please contact [Name] at [email].

I'll respond to your email when I return on [return date].

Thanks,
[Name]
```
