# Meeting Invite Templates

## Standard Meeting Invite

```
SUBJECT: [Meeting Type]: [Specific Topic/Decision]

LOCATION: [Room name / Video link]

DESCRIPTION:
-----------
Purpose:
[One sentence on why we're meeting and what we'll decide]

Agenda:
1. [Topic 1] - [Time] - [Owner]
2. [Topic 2] - [Time] - [Owner]
3. [Topic 3] - [Time] - [Owner]

Pre-Work:
- [ ] [Preparation task 1]
- [ ] [Review document: link]

Expected Outcome:
[What should we have decided/accomplished by end]

Notes Doc: [Link to collaborative doc]
```

---

## Decision Meeting

```
SUBJECT: DECISION: [What we're deciding]

PURPOSE:
[Specific decision to be made]

BACKGROUND:
[2-3 sentences of context]

AGENDA:
1. Context review (5 min) - [Presenter]
2. Present options (10 min) - [Presenter]
3. Discussion (15 min) - All
4. Decision (5 min) - All

PRE-WORK:
- Review: [Options document] (link)
- Come prepared with your recommendation

EXPECTED OUTCOME:
Approved decision with owner and next steps documented.
```

## Status/Sync Meeting

```
SUBJECT: [Weekly/Biweekly] [Team/Project] Sync

PURPOSE:
Align on progress, blockers, and priorities.

STANDING AGENDA:
1. Wins from last period (5 min) - Round robin
2. Blockers and help needed (10 min) - As needed
3. This period's priorities (10 min) - Round robin
4. Announcements (5 min) - Anyone

PRE-WORK:
- Update your section in the Status Doc (link)

NOTES:
- This meeting will be cancelled if no blockers or updates
- Async updates preferred when possible
```

## Brainstorm Meeting

```
SUBJECT: BRAINSTORM: [Topic]

PURPOSE:
Generate ideas for [topic]. No bad ideas!

AGENDA:
1. Context setting (5 min) - [Facilitator]
2. Silent brainstorm (10 min) - All (sticky notes/Miro)
3. Share and cluster (15 min) - All
4. Dot voting (5 min) - All
5. Discuss top ideas (10 min) - All

PRE-WORK:
- Think about: [Prompt questions]
- Review: [Background material]

RULES:
- No criticism during brainstorm
- Build on others' ideas ("Yes, and...")
- Quantity over quality initially

OUTCOME:
Top 5 ideas ranked for further exploration.
```

## 1:1 Meeting

```
SUBJECT: 1:1: [Name] / [Name]

RECURRING: [Frequency]

FORMAT:
This is your meeting - bring topics you want to discuss.

STANDING AGENDA:
1. Your updates and wins
2. Challenges and blockers
3. Feedback (both directions)
4. Career/development topics
5. Anything else

SHARED DOC:
[Link to running 1:1 doc]

Add your topics to the doc before our meeting.
```

## Tips for Calendar Invites

1. **Subject line matters**: Be specific so people can triage
2. **Include the "why"**: Purpose helps people prepare
3. **Add the video link**: Don't make people search
4. **Time allocation**: Shows you've thought it through
5. **Pre-work is optional**: Not every meeting needs it
6. **Expected outcome**: Keeps everyone focused
7. **Shared notes doc**: Creates accountability
