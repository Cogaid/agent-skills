## Purpose

Create personalized, high-converting cold outreach messages that capture attention and generate meetings.

## Quick Reference

### Cold Email Performance Benchmarks

| Metric | Average | Good | Excellent |
|--------|---------|------|-----------|
| Open Rate | 20% | 35% | 50%+ |
| Reply Rate | 1-2% | 5% | 10%+ |
| Meeting Rate | 0.5% | 2% | 5%+ |

### Outreach Channels

| Channel | Best For | Response Time | Personalization |
|---------|----------|---------------|-----------------|
| Email | All audiences | 1-3 days | Medium-High |
| LinkedIn | B2B professionals | Same day | High |
| Phone | High-value targets | Immediate | Highest |
| Video | Standing out | 1-2 days | Highest |

## Cold Email Framework (AIDA-P)

### Structure

| Element | Purpose | Length |
|---------|---------|--------|
| **A**ttention | Subject line hook | 3-7 words |
| **I**nterest | Personalized opening | 1-2 sentences |
| **D**esire | Value proposition | 2-3 sentences |
| **A**ction | Clear CTA | 1 sentence |
| **P**.S. | Second hook (optional) | 1 sentence |

### Email Blueprint

```
SUBJECT: [Attention-grabbing, personalized]

Hi [First Name],

[INTEREST: Personalized observation about them/their company]

[DESIRE: Pain point + how you help + brief proof]

[ACTION: Single, clear, low-friction ask]

[Signature]

P.S. [Optional second hook or social proof]
```

## Subject Line Formulas

### High-Converting Patterns

| Pattern | Example | Use When |
|---------|---------|----------|
| Question | "Quick question about [initiative]?" | Opening curiosity |
| Mutual connection | "[Referral name] suggested I reach out" | Have a referral |
| Observation | "Noticed [specific thing]" | Research-based |
| Value-first | "[Outcome] for [Company]" | Clear value prop |
| Pattern interrupt | "[Emoji] [Unexpected phrase]" | Standing out |

### Examples by Industry

**SaaS:**
- "Scaling [their tech stack]?"
- "Saw your [ProductHunt/funding] news"
- "Quick idea for [Company]"

**Services:**
- "Thoughts on [their strategy]?"
- "[Competitor] is doing this..."
- "Re: [Industry trend]"

**Enterprise:**
- "[Mutual connection] introduction"
- "Following up from [event]"
- "For [Name] at [Company]"

## Personalization Playbook

### Research Checklist

```
Before Writing:
□ LinkedIn profile (role, recent posts, career history)
□ Company website (about, news, careers page)
□ Recent news/press releases
□ Twitter/social media
□ Podcast appearances or interviews
□ Blog posts they've written
□ Mutual connections
□ Tech stack (BuiltWith, Wappalyzer)
□ Job postings (indicates priorities)
□ Funding/growth news
```

### Personalization Levels

| Level | Effort | Example | Reply Lift |
|-------|--------|---------|------------|
| None | 0 | Generic template | Baseline |
| Light | 1 min | Company name + role | +20% |
| Medium | 5 min | Recent news + insight | +50% |
| Deep | 15 min | Specific observation + connection | +100% |

## Templates

### Template 1: The Observation
```
SUBJECT: Noticed [specific observation]

Hi [Name],

I saw that [specific observation about their company/role - be genuine].

[Companies in your space/People in your role] often struggle with [pain point].

We've helped [similar company] [achieve specific result].

Worth a quick chat to see if we could help [Company]?

[Name]
```

### Template 2: The Trigger Event
```
SUBJECT: Congrats on [trigger event]

Hi [Name],

Saw the news about [trigger event - funding, new role, launch, etc.]. Congrats!

When [similar companies/leaders] hit this stage, [common challenge] usually becomes a priority.

We help [type of company] [achieve outcome] without [common pain].

[Short proof point or name drop]

Would it make sense to chat for 15 minutes?

[Name]
```

### Template 3: The Mutual Connection
```
SUBJECT: [Mutual connection] suggested we connect

Hi [Name],

[Mutual connection] mentioned you might be interested in [topic/outcome].

We've been helping them [specific result] at [their company].

They thought you might benefit from a similar approach at [Company].

Open to a quick conversation?

[Name]
```

### Template 4: The Problem-Solution
```
SUBJECT: Quick thought on [their initiative/challenge]

Hi [Name],

[Question about their known challenge]?

We work with [similar companies] who face this exact issue.

[One sentence on your solution + result]

Example: We helped [Company] [specific metric].

Worth 15 minutes to see if this could work for [Company]?

[Name]
```

### Template 5: The Video Intro
```
SUBJECT: Made this for you, [Name] 🎥

Hi [Name],

Recorded a quick 60-second video with an idea for [Company]:

[Video link/thumbnail]

Let me know if it resonates.

[Name]
```

## Follow-Up Sequence

### Timing

| Touch | Timing | Focus |
|-------|--------|-------|
| Email 1 | Day 0 | Initial outreach |
| Email 2 | Day 3 | New angle/value |
| Email 3 | Day 7 | Social proof |
| Email 4 | Day 14 | Breakup email |
| LinkedIn | Day 2 | Connection request |

### Follow-Up Templates

**Follow-Up 1 (Day 3):**
```
SUBJECT: Re: [Original subject]

Hi [Name],

Wanted to make sure this didn't get buried.

[Add new piece of value - article, insight, case study]

Any interest in chatting?

[Name]
```

**Follow-Up 2 (Day 7):**
```
SUBJECT: [Competitor] just did this

Hi [Name],

Quick update - [Competitor/similar company] just [achieved result with us].

Thought you'd want to know given [your role/initiative].

15 minutes sometime this week?

[Name]
```

**Breakup Email (Day 14):**
```
SUBJECT: Should I close your file?

Hi [Name],

I've reached out a few times but haven't heard back.

No worries if the timing isn't right - I'll assume [solution] isn't a priority now.

If things change, I'm here: [calendar link]

[Name]
```

## Best Practices

### Do's
- Research before writing
- Personalize the first line
- Keep it under 100 words
- Focus on them, not you
- Have one clear CTA
- Send at optimal times (Tue-Thu, 8-10am)
- A/B test subject lines
- Track everything

### Don'ts
- Use "Dear Sir/Madam"
- Lead with your product
- Write walls of text
- Use multiple CTAs
- Sound desperate
- Lie or mislead
- Forget to proofread
- Send from noreply@
