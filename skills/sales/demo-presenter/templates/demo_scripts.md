# Demo Scripts by Audience

## Executive Demo Script (20-25 min)

### Opening (3 min)

```
"[Name], thank you for taking the time today. Before we dive in,
I want to make sure this is valuable for you.

From our earlier conversations, it sounds like [Company] is focused on
[strategic priority]. Is that still the main driver?

[Wait for confirmation/clarification]

Perfect. What I'd like to do is show you three things:

1. How [solution] addresses [their priority] directly
2. What results similar companies have achieved
3. A quick look at how it would work for your team

Does that work? Is there anything else you'd specifically like me to cover?

[Adjust agenda based on response]

Great - let's jump in."
```

### Discovery Checkpoint (2 min)

```
"Before I show you the platform, a quick question:

When you think about [their priority], what does success look like
for [Company] 12 months from now?

[Listen carefully - this shapes the entire demo]

Got it. So if we could help you [their success definition],
that would be meaningful?

Let me show you exactly how we do that."
```

### Demo Body (12-15 min)

**Section 1: The Big Picture (3 min)**
```
"Let me start with the executive dashboard - this is where
you'd see [Company]'s real-time [relevant metrics].

[Show dashboard]

What you're looking at is:
• [Metric 1] - This shows [explanation]
• [Metric 2] - This tracks [explanation]
• [Metric 3] - This measures [explanation]

Companies like [similar company] typically see [improvement] here
within [timeframe].

Does this kind of visibility match what you had in mind?"
```

**Section 2: The Key Capability (5 min)**
```
"Now let me show you the capability that directly addresses
[their stated priority].

[Navigate to feature]

Here's how this works:
[Demonstrate 3-4 key actions]

The reason this matters for [Company] is [specific benefit].

[Relevant company] was dealing with a similar challenge.
After implementing this, they saw [specific result].

What questions do you have about this?"
```

**Section 3: The Easy Win (4 min)**
```
"One more thing I want to show you - this is often what
gets teams excited.

[Navigate to easy-win feature]

This [feature] lets your team [benefit].

In your case, this could mean [specific application].

I'm curious - how much time does your team currently spend on [related task]?"

[Listen for pain point validation]
```

### Closing (3-5 min)

```
"Based on what we've discussed, it sounds like [solution] could
help [Company]:

1. [Restate benefit matching their priority 1]
2. [Restate benefit matching their priority 2]
3. [Restate benefit matching their priority 3]

What's your initial reaction?

[Listen for feedback/concerns]

Typically, at this point, companies like to [suggested next step].

Would that make sense as a next step?"
```

## Manager Demo Script (30-40 min)

### Opening (5 min)

```
"Thanks for joining today, [Name]. I know you're busy managing
[their team/function], so I want to make sure this is worth your time.

Quick question: What's taking up most of your team's time right now
that you wish you could streamline?

[Listen carefully]

That's exactly what I want to focus on today.

Here's my plan:
1. Show you how [solution] would fit into your current workflow
2. Walk through a day-in-the-life scenario for your team
3. Discuss what implementation and adoption looks like
4. Answer your questions

Does that cover what you need? Anything to add?"
```

### Workflow Demo (15-20 min)

**Current State Acknowledgment**
```
"Before I show you the platform, tell me - what does your team's
current process look like for [relevant task]?

[Map their current workflow]

So if I understand correctly:
1. [Step 1]
2. [Step 2]
3. [Step 3]

Where does it usually break down or slow down?"
```

**The "Day in the Life"**
```
"Let me show you how this would work for [role on their team].

It's 8:30 AM. They log in and see:
[Show dashboard]

Their first task is [common task]. Here's how they'd do it:
[Demonstrate workflow]

Notice how [efficiency feature]? That step that used to take
[time] now takes [reduced time].

By lunchtime, they've:
• [Accomplishment 1]
• [Accomplishment 2]
• [Accomplishment 3]

How does that compare to today?"
```

**Team Features**
```
"Now, from your perspective as a manager:
[Show manager view]

You can see:
• [What team is working on]
• [Performance metrics]
• [Bottlenecks or issues]

This means instead of asking for updates, you have real-time visibility.

What reports do you currently have to run manually?"
```

### Adoption & Implementation (8-10 min)

```
"I know one of your concerns is probably adoption -
getting your team to actually use this.

Here's how we handle that:

Training:
• [Training approach] - typically [time]
• [Support resources]
• [Champions/super users]

Implementation:
• Phase 1: [Details] - [timeframe]
• Phase 2: [Details] - [timeframe]
• Phase 3: [Details] - [timeframe]

Your dedicated [CSM/support person] handles all of this.

What concerns do you have about your team adopting something new?"
```

### Closing (5 min)

```
"To recap, here's what we covered:

1. [Workflow improvement] - This would save your team [time/effort]
2. [Manager visibility] - You'd have real-time insight into [metrics]
3. [Adoption support] - We'd have you up and running in [timeframe]

What's your gut reaction?

[Listen]

The logical next step would be [suggested action].
When would make sense for that?"
```

## Technical Demo Script (45-60 min)

### Opening (5 min)

```
"[Name], thanks for making time for this deeper dive.
I know you'll want to understand the technical details.

Before we get into architecture and integrations,
help me understand your current stack so I can focus on
what's most relevant.

What's your primary [relevant system]?
What integrations are must-haves?
Any specific security or compliance requirements?"
```

### Architecture Overview (10 min)

```
"Let me start with how [solution] is built.

[Show architecture diagram]

At a high level:
• Infrastructure: [cloud provider, deployment model]
• Data layer: [database, storage]
• API layer: [REST/GraphQL, rate limits]
• Frontend: [technology, delivery]

Security-wise:
• Encryption: [at rest, in transit]
• Authentication: [SSO, MFA]
• Compliance: [certifications]

What questions do you have about architecture?"
```

### Integration Deep Dive (15 min)

```
"Now let me show you how we integrate with [their critical system].

[Show integration/API documentation]

We have a [native integration / API connector] for [system].

Here's how data flows:
1. [Step 1]
2. [Step 2]
3. [Step 3]

Real-time sync happens [frequency].

Let me show you this in action:
[Demonstrate live integration]

For custom integrations, here's our API:
[Show API documentation]

What specific data do you need to flow between systems?"
```

### Security & Compliance (10 min)

```
"Let's talk security - I know this is critical for your evaluation.

Access controls:
• [RBAC details]
• [Permission levels]
• [Audit logging]

Data handling:
• [Where data is stored]
• [Data residency options]
• [Backup/recovery]

Compliance:
• [Certifications held]
• [Audit reports available]
• [Penetration testing]

Here's our security whitepaper: [share link]

What specific requirements do you need to meet?"
```

### Q&A and Edge Cases (10-15 min)

```
"I've covered the main areas - what haven't I addressed?

[Be prepared for technical deep-dive questions]

If you want to get into specifics on:
• [Topic 1] - I can show you [resource]
• [Topic 2] - Our engineering team can do a deeper call
• [Topic 3] - Here's documentation [share]

What else is on your evaluation checklist?"
```

### Technical Next Steps (5 min)

```
"From a technical evaluation standpoint, here's what I suggest:

1. I'll send you [documentation/security questionnaire]
2. We can arrange an [engineering call / POC / trial]
3. [Any other technical validation]

What would be most helpful for your technical review?

Is there anyone else on your team who should be involved?"
```

## Discovery Demo Script (For Early-Stage Prospects)

### Opening (5 min)

```
"Thanks for taking time to learn about [solution].

Before I show you anything, I'd love to understand what
brought you to us today.

What are you trying to accomplish?
What's not working with your current approach?
What does success look like?"
```

### Lightweight Demo (15 min)

```
"Based on what you shared, let me show you a few things
that directly relate to [their stated need].

I'll keep this high-level - we can go deeper on
anything that catches your interest.

[Show 2-3 relevant capabilities, pausing for reaction]

What questions do you have so far?
What would you want to explore further?"
```

### Qualification and Next Steps (10 min)

```
"It sounds like [solution] could help with [their need].

A few questions to see if there's a fit:

• What's your timeline for addressing this?
• Who else would be involved in evaluating solutions?
• What budget have you allocated for this?
• What else are you considering?

[Based on qualification]

Here's what I'd suggest as a next step..."
```
