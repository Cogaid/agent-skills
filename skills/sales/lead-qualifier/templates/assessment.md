## Tips for Assessments

1. **Be honest** - Don't inflate scores to keep deals
2. **Use evidence** - Back up scores with what they said
3. **Update regularly** - Reassess after each interaction
4. **Act on gaps** - Low scores = action items to address
5. **Share with team** - Get second opinions on key deals
