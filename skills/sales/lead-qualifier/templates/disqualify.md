# Disqualification Templates

## Professional Disqualification Scripts

### Not a Fit - General

```
Hi [Name],

Thanks for taking the time to explore [Your Solution] with us.

Based on our conversations, I don't think we're the best fit for
[Company] right now. [Specific reason - be honest but kind].

Here's what I'd suggest instead:
- [Alternative solution that might fit better]
- [Resource that could help them]

If [specific condition that would change the fit], please reach out -
we'd love to help then.

Best,
[Your name]
```

---

### Timing Not Right

```
Hi [Name],

I appreciate the conversations we've had about [topic].

It sounds like [Q2/next year/after the merger] is better timing
for this initiative. I completely understand - timing matters.

Here's what I suggest:
1. I'll set a reminder to reach out in [timeframe]
2. In the meantime, [relevant resource] might be helpful
3. If anything changes sooner, you know where to find me

Looking forward to reconnecting when the time is right.

Best,
[Your name]
```

---

### Budget Mismatch

```
Hi [Name],

Thanks for being transparent about budget constraints.

Our solution starts at $[X], which I understand is above your
current range. Rather than try to force a fit, here are some
options that might work better:

1. [Lower-cost alternative]
2. [Different approach to their problem]
3. [Way to build the business case for budget]

If budget becomes available or priorities shift, I'd welcome
the chance to continue our conversation.

Best,
[Your name]
```

### Missing Required Features

```

we currently support, and it's not on our near-term roadmap.

I want to be upfront rather than overpromise. For [requirement],

If [your need changes] or [we build this feature], I'll reach out.
In the meantime, I wish you the best in finding the right solution.

Best,
[Your name]
```

## Quick Disqualification Checklist

Before disqualifying, confirm:

- [ ] Explored all options (phased approach, smaller scope, etc.)
- [ ] Understood their real constraints (not just stated ones)
- [ ] Offered alternatives or resources
- [ ] Left door open for future engagement
- [ ] Documented reason in CRM
- [ ] Sent professional close-out message

## Disqualification Best Practices

1. **Be direct but kind** - They deserve honesty
2. **Give specific reasons** - "Not a fit" is too vague
3. **Offer alternatives** - Help them even if you can't sell to them
4. **Leave the door open** - Situations change
5. **Document thoroughly** - Future you needs to know why
6. **Don't burn bridges** - They might refer someone who IS a fit
