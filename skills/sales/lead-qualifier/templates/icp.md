## Tips for ICP Development

1. **Start with data** - Analyze your best customers
2. **Include negatives** - What makes a bad customer?
3. **Get specific** - Vague ICPs are useless
4. **Validate continuously** - Update based on wins/losses
5. **Align sales & marketing** - Everyone uses same ICP
6. **Document why** - Not just "50+ employees" but why that matters
