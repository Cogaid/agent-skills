# Follow-Up Email Sequence Templates

## Cold Outreach Sequence (6-Touch)

### Touch 1: The Opener

**Subject Options:**
- Quick question about [their priority]
- [Mutual connection] suggested I reach out
- [Company]'s [specific initiative]?

**Email:**
```
Hi [Name],

I noticed [Company] recently [trigger: raised funding/launched product/expanded/hired].

Companies going through similar [growth/change/expansion] often tell us
[specific challenge relevant to their situation].

Curious if that's on your radar?

If so, might be worth a quick chat to share what's worked for
[similar company in their space].

Either way, congrats on [trigger event].

[Signature]
```

---

### Touch 2: Value Add (Day 3)

**Subject Options:**
- Re: [previous subject]
- Thought this was relevant
- Quick resource for [their challenge]

**Email:**
```
[Name],

Following up on my note from earlier this week.

In the meantime, thought you might find this useful:

[Link to relevant, non-promotional content]

It covers [brief description of what they'll learn].

Given [Company]'s focus on [their area], seemed relevant.

Happy to discuss how this applies to your situation.

[Signature]
```

---

### Touch 3: Different Channel Context (Day 7)

**Subject Options:**
- Worth 10 minutes?
- One more try
- Different angle on [topic]

**Email:**
```
Hi [Name],

Tried connecting on LinkedIn as well – no worries if you
missed it in the noise.

Different angle: [Similar company] was dealing with [specific challenge]
when we started working together.

Within [timeframe], they saw [specific metric improvement].

Would it be valuable to hear how they approached it?

If you're the wrong person for this, no problem – just point
me in the right direction.

[Signature]
```

---

### Touch 4: Social Proof (Day 12)

**Subject Options:**
- How [Similar Company] solved [challenge]
- Quick case study
- [Metric improvement] at [Similar Company]

**Email:**
```
[Name],

I know you're busy, so I'll keep this short.

[Similar Company] came to us with [challenge similar to prospect's].

Here's what happened:
• [Result 1 - specific metric]
• [Result 2 - specific metric]
• [Result 3 - specific metric]

Full story here: [case study link] (2-min read)

If any of this sounds relevant to [Company], let's find
15 minutes to explore.

[Signature]
```

---

### Touch 5: Direct Ask (Day 16)

**Subject Options:**
- Next Tuesday or Thursday?
- 15 minutes this week?
- Let's make this easy

**Email:**
```
Hi [Name],

I've reached out a few times – I get it, priorities.

Here's my simple ask:

15 minutes to discuss whether [solving their challenge]
is something [Company] is actively working on.

If yes, I'll share exactly how we've helped [similar companies]
and you can decide if it's worth exploring further.

If not, I'll stop reaching out.

Does [Day] at [Time] or [Day] at [Time] work?

[Signature]
```

---

### Touch 6: Breakup (Day 21)

**Subject Options:**
- Should I close your file?
- Closing the loop
- Not a priority?

**Email:**
```
[Name],

I've tried reaching you a few times about [topic].

I'm guessing either:

a) This isn't a priority right now
b) You're the wrong person for this
c) You've been swamped

No hard feelings if any of the above.

I'll assume [topic] isn't a fit and won't keep reaching out.

If anything changes, you know where to find me.

Wishing you all the best,
[Signature]
```

## Post-Meeting Follow-Up Sequence

### Same Day: Thank You

**Subject:** Great chatting, [Name] - next steps inside

**Email:**
```
Hi [Name],

Really enjoyed our conversation today about [specific topic discussed].

As promised, here's:
• [Resource/document mentioned]
• [Additional helpful content]

Key takeaways from our discussion:
1. [Their challenge/goal]
2. [Potential solution/approach]
3. [Agreed next step]

I'll [next action you're taking] by [date].

In the meantime, let me know if any questions come up.

Talk soon,
[Signature]
```

---

### Touch 2: Value Delivery (Day 2)

**Subject:** Resource for [their specific challenge]

**Email:**
```
[Name],

Following up on our conversation about [specific challenge].

I pulled together [resource type] that addresses
exactly what you mentioned about [their specific situation].

Here it is: [Link/Attachment]

The section on [specific part] is most relevant to what
[Company] is working on.

Happy to walk through it together if useful.

[Signature]
```

---

### Touch 3: Stakeholder Prep (Day 5)

**Subject:** Help for your internal conversation

**Email:**
```
Hi [Name],

As you're discussing [topic] with [stakeholders/team],
thought this might help:

I've put together a one-pager that covers:
• The problem and impact
• Our approach
• Expected outcomes
• [Similar company] results

[Link/Attachment]

Feel free to share with [stakeholder names if known].

Would it help if I joined that conversation? Happy to
answer technical questions directly.

[Signature]
```

---

### Touch 4: Progress Check (Day 9)

**Subject:** Quick check-in on [topic]

**Email:**
```

Wanted to check in on how conversations about [topic]
are progressing internally.

A few things that might help:

We're holding [specific date/resource] for [Company] –
just let me know if you need more time.

What's the best next step?

[Signature]
```

## Re-Engagement Sequence

### Touch 1: The Check-In

**Subject:** [Topic] still on your radar?

**Email:**
```
Hi [Name],

It's been [X months] since we last discussed
[specific topic from previous conversation].

At the time, you were exploring [their initiative/goal].

Curious where that stands now?

No pressure – just wanted to see if the timing
is better to revisit the conversation.

[Signature]
```

---

### Touch 2: New Development

**Subject:** Something's changed since we last spoke

**Email:**
```
[Name],

A lot has happened since our last conversation:

• [New capability/feature]
• [New customer win in their space]
• [Market development relevant to them]

Given [Company]'s focus on [their priority], one
of these might be relevant.

Worth a quick catch-up to see?

[Signature]
```

---

### Touch 3: Content Trigger

**Subject:** Saw this and thought of you

**Email:**
```
[Name],

Came across this [article/report/content] and
immediately thought of our conversation about [topic].

[Link]

The part about [specific section] relates directly
to what you mentioned about [their challenge].

Would love to hear your take if you get a chance
to check it out.

[Signature]
```

## Lost Deal Revival Sequence

### Touch 1: The Humble Check-In (30 days post-loss)

**Subject:** How's [alternative solution] working out?

**Email:**
```
Hi [Name],

Wanted to check in since you went with [competitor/alternative].

Genuinely curious: How's it going?

If everything's great, I'm happy for you.

If there have been any surprises, I'm here to chat –
no sales pitch, just an honest conversation.

[Signature]
```

---

### Touch 2: New Value Prop (60 days post-loss)

**Subject:** We've made some changes

**Email:**
```
[Name],

Since we last spoke, we've addressed [the objection/concern they raised]:

• [Improvement 1]
• [Improvement 2]
• [Improvement 3]

If [original concern] was the deciding factor, it might
be worth a fresh look.

No pressure – but if you're curious, I'm happy to
do a quick walkthrough of what's changed.

[Signature]
```

---

### Touch 3: Industry Trigger (90 days post-loss)

**Subject:** [Industry change] impact on [Company]

**Email:**
```
[Name],

[Industry development/regulatory change/market shift] has
been impacting a lot of [their industry] companies.

We've been helping [similar companies] navigate this by
[specific approach].

Given [Company]'s position, thought this might be relevant.

Open to a quick conversation if it would be helpful.

[Signature]
```

## LinkedIn Message Sequences

```
Hi [Name] – I work with [similar companies] helping them
[achieve result]. Saw [trigger/connection point] and
thought we should connect. No pitch, just networking.
```

### Post-Connection Message 1

```
Thanks for connecting, [Name]!

I noticed [Company] is [doing something relevant].
We've helped [similar companies] with [related challenge].

Thought it might be worth a quick chat?
Either way, happy to be connected.
```

### Post-Connection Message 2

```
Hi [Name] – shared something relevant to [their area]
on my profile. Check it out if you get a chance.

Also noticed you [their recent activity]. Would love
to hear more about [topic].

Open to a quick call this week?
```
