## Presenting ROI Effectively

### Key Points:
1. Use **their numbers** from discovery, not industry averages
2. Be **conservative** in estimates (builds credibility)
3. Show the **math** - don't just state the result
4. Include **payback period** - executives care about this
5. Add **qualitative benefits** they mentioned
6. Compare to **cost of inaction**, not just status quo

### Common Mistakes:
- Overly optimistic projections (use 50-70% of best case)
- Forgetting ongoing costs
- Not accounting for ramp-up time
- Using benefits they didn't mention as priorities
