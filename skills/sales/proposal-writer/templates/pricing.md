## Pricing Presentation Tips

### Do:
- Lead with value, not price
- Show the math behind ROI
- Make the recommended option clear
- Include payment flexibility
- Round to clean numbers when possible

### Don't:
- Hide the price at the end
- Use complex pricing structures
- Forget to include what's NOT included
- Present more than 3-4 options
- Make them calculate totals

### Framing Language:
- "Investment" not "Cost"
- "What you receive" not "What we do"
- "Return" not "Savings"
- "Partners with" not "Clients"
