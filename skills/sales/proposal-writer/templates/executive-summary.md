# Executive Summary Templates

## Standard Template

```markdown
# Executive Summary

## Situation

[Company Name] is currently facing [1-2 specific challenges from discovery].
This is resulting in [quantified business impact they mentioned].

## Opportunity

Your goal is to [desired outcome in their words] by [their timeline].
Successfully achieving this would mean [positive implications they shared].

## Proposed Solution

We recommend [high-level solution] that will enable you to:

• **[Outcome 1]**: [Quantified result, e.g., "Reduce processing time by 40%"]
• **[Outcome 2]**: [Quantified result]
• **[Outcome 3]**: [Quantified result]

## Expected Results

Based on our work with [similar company/industry], we expect:

| Metric | Current | Expected | Timeline |
|--------|---------|----------|----------|
| [Metric 1] | [Current value] | [Target value] | [Timeframe] |
| [Metric 2] | [Current value] | [Target value] | [Timeframe] |

**Projected ROI**: [X]% in Year 1

## Investment

**Total Investment**: $[Amount] - $[Amount] depending on [scope variable]

**Timeline**: [Duration] from kick-off to full deployment

## Next Step

To discuss this proposal and address any questions, [Name] will follow up
on [specific date] or schedule a call at [calendar link].
```

## Tips for Executive Summaries

1. **Keep it to 1 page** - Decision-makers skim
2. **Lead with their problem** - Not your company
3. **Use numbers** - Quantify everything possible
4. **Include a visual** - Table or chart helps scanning
5. **End with clear next step** - Don't leave them wondering
6. **Use their words** - Build credibility by referencing discovery
