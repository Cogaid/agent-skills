# Discovery Call Follow-Up Templates

## Standard Follow-Up

```
Subject: Following up from our conversation

Hi [Name],

Thanks for taking the time to chat today. I enjoyed learning about
[something specific they shared].

As I understood it, your main priorities are:
1. [Priority/challenge 1]
2. [Priority/challenge 2]
3. [Priority/challenge 3]

Based on that, I think [brief value proposition tied to their needs].

As discussed, next steps:
- [Action 1 - who, what, when]
- [Action 2 - who, what, when]

I'm looking forward to [next meeting] on [date/time].

Best,
[Your name]
```

---

## Follow-Up with Materials

```
Subject: Materials from our conversation + next steps

Hi [Name],

Thanks again for our conversation today.

As promised, here are the materials we discussed:
- [Document 1]: [Brief description]
- [Document 2]: [Brief description]
- [Case study]: How [similar company] achieved [result]

Based on what you shared about [their challenge], I'd highlight
[specific section or page] as most relevant to your situation.

Our next step is [next meeting] on [date/time]. In the meantime,
feel free to share these with [other stakeholders mentioned].

Let me know if any questions come up.

Best,
[Your name]
```

---

## Follow-Up After Great Call

```
Subject: Excited about what we discussed

Hi [Name],

I really enjoyed our conversation today - it's clear you've thought
deeply about [topic they discussed].

What stood out to me:
- [Insight 1 they shared]
- [Insight 2 they shared]

I'm confident we can help you [achieve outcome], and I'm looking
forward to exploring that further on [next meeting date].

In the meantime, I thought you might find this interesting:
[Relevant article/case study/resource]

Talk soon,
[Your name]
```

---

## Follow-Up When Next Steps Were Unclear

```
Subject: Clarifying our next steps

Hi [Name],

Thanks for our conversation today. I wanted to follow up to clarify
next steps.

If I understood correctly, you'd like to:
- [Possible next step 1]
- [Possible next step 2]

What would be most helpful for you at this point? I'm happy to
[option A] or [option B] - whatever moves things forward.

Let me know your preference and I'll get that set up.

Best,
[Your name]
```

---

## Usage Tips

1. **Send within 1 hour** of the call
2. **Reference specific things** they said
3. **Use their words**, not yours
4. **Keep it short** - they're busy
5. **End with clear next step**
