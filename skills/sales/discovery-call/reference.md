## SPIN Question Library

### Situation Questions

*Use sparingly - you should know most of this from research*

**Current State**:
- "Walk me through your current process for [area]..."
- "What tools/systems are you using today?"
- "Who's involved in [process] currently?"
- "How long have you been doing it this way?"

**Team & Structure**:
- "How is your team organized?"
- "Who handles [responsibility] today?"
- "What does your tech stack look like?"

---

### Problem Questions

**General Discovery**:
- "What's working well? What's not?"
- "Where are you spending more time than you'd like?"
- "What happens when [problem scenario]?"
- "What have you tried before?"
- "Why do you think that didn't work?"

**Pain Identification**:
- "What's the biggest challenge you're facing with [area]?"
- "What keeps you up at night about [topic]?"
- "If you could wave a magic wand, what would you fix?"
- "What's frustrating about your current approach?"

**Process Gaps**:
- "Where do things break down?"
- "What manual work could be automated?"
- "Where do you lose visibility?"

---

### Implication Questions

**Business Impact**:
- "How does that affect your [revenue/team/customers]?"
- "What's the cost of this problem?"
- "If this continues, what happens in 6-12 months?"
- "How does this impact your ability to [goal]?"

**Ripple Effects**:
- "How does this affect other teams?"
- "What opportunities are you missing because of this?"
- "How much time does your team spend on workarounds?"

**Quantifying Pain**:
- "Can you put a number on that?"
- "How many hours/dollars is this costing?"
- "What's the opportunity cost?"

---

### Need-Payoff Questions

**Envisioning Success**:
- "If you could solve this, what would change?"
- "What would it mean for your team to [desired state]?"
- "How would [outcome] impact [metric they care about]?"
- "What would you do with the time/money you'd save?"

**Value Confirmation**:
- "Would that be valuable to you?"
- "How would that help you achieve [their goal]?"
- "Is that something worth solving?"

## Call Structure

### Opening (5 min)

1. **Rapport**: Reference something from research
2. **Agenda**: "Here's what I'd like to cover..."
3. **Permission**: "I'll ask some questions - is that okay?"

### Discovery (20-30 min)

1. Start with Situation (brief)
2. Move to Problems (spend time here)
3. Explore Implications (amplify pain)
4. Confirm with Need-Payoff

**Key rules**:
- Listen more than talk (70/30)
- Take notes on exact words
- Ask follow-ups: "Tell me more..."
- Embrace silence

### Solution Preview (5-10 min)

Only after thorough discovery:
- Connect their problems to your solution
- Use relevant case studies
- Keep high-level, leave them wanting more

### Close (5 min)

- Check for fit: "Based on our conversation..."
- Address concerns
- Secure next step with calendar invite
